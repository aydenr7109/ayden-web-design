Differences between Smash 4 and Ultimate


Super Smash bros for WiiU and Super Smash Bros Ultimate may be somewhat similarly designed given that there are entries in the Super Smash Bros series but, they have a number of differences that make a player choose one over the other.

  1. Ultimate's shading make the appearance of certain characters look not only better than Smash 4 but also impressive given the Switch's limits.
  2. The speed of all characters is noticeably faster in Ultimate than in Smash 4 which might interest competitive players but push away casual players.
  3. Ultimate has certain elements that make it more intense and epic such as a slow motion effect on strong attacks and a shield that is faster to break when compared to the other games.
